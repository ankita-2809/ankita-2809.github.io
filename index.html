About Me

Hello there,
I am an instructional designer with a passion for creating engaging and effective e-learning courses. With experience designing and developing courses at all levels, from K-12 to corporate compliance training, I have honed my skills in a range of instructional design strategies, including gamification, microlearning, and scenario-based learning. I am well-versed in conducting needs analysis, adhering to WCAG guidelines, and using AI tools for both creation and research purposes.
Over the years, I have had the opportunity to work with a variety of authoring tools such as Rise, Storyline, Camtasia, Slides.com, Comic Life, Canva, PowerPoint, Lectora, Adobe Captivate, and many more. Additionally, I have used cutting-edge AI tools such as chatGPT, Midjourney5, Dall-e, and Resemble to create engaging and immersive learning experiences for my learners.
Whether working as a freelancer or full-time employee, my focus has always been on delivering high-quality, effective courses that meet the needs of my clients and learners. With a hunger for knowledge and a drive to continuously improve, I am always on the lookout for new tools and techniques that can help me streamline my workflow without sacrificing the quality of my deliverables.
If you have any questions or would like to learn more about my work as an instructional designer, please don't hesitate to reach out!
